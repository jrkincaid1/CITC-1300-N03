Welcome to Monster Hunter Hunters!

Monster Hunter Hunters is a site dedicated to fans of Capcom's Monster Hunter series. It's a series all about hunting down giant monsters and using the parts you carve from them to build weapons and armor to take down bigger and badder monsters!

At Monster Hunter Hunters, we aim to arm new hunters with the knowledge they need to hit the road running with monster facts and hunting tips so they can begin playig as soon as possible without getting overwhelmed by all the game's many systems. We strive to be a dedicated gaming community where you can come have a chat with like-minded hunters and feel welcome and at home!
