Hyrule's Unexplained

Welcome to Hyrule's Unexplained!

Here, we like to take a look at some of the weird (and often criminally unexplained) aspects of the Legend of Zelda series. From where different types of enemies come from to the origins of the dungeons and the Zonai tribe, you'll find discussion of it here! Thanks for taking the time to drop by!

Below is a table of common Zelda titles that have plenty of unexplained mysteries to them. These mysteries can pertain to the timeline, occurences within the game, or anything in between!
