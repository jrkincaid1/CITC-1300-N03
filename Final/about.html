About Hyrule's Unexplained

Welcome to Hyrule's Unexplained! Here, we take a look at some of the weirder and more mysterious sides of the Zelda franchise. Have you ever wondered who mapped out the dungeons (and thought to leave the map inside the dungeon)? How about what creature left the huge claw marks in the tree at Lake Hylia? We seek to answer these mysteries and many more. Bear in mind that our ideas are only theories unless proven as fact from the devs themselves.

Hyrule's Unexplained is the rebirth of a site that I had back in January of 2010. One of my favorite aspects of the Zelda series are the unexplained mysteries that proliferate the games. I wanted a place to write some theories and ideas and present them to curious readers as articles. I even setup some forums as a place for discussion. While the site never took off like I'd hoped, it was a lot of fun and I met some awesome people. This new and improved site is like a love song to my original idea. Here's to you, 2010 me. Let's get this ball rolling again!
