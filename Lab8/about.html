About Monster Hunter Buddy Finder

Monster Hunter Buddy Finder is a tool for Monster Hunter Rise players on the Nintendo Switch to locate like-minded Rise fans to hunt with. We ask for the username, friend code, location, and available play times so that you can match up with someone that is available when you are.

We will never ask for passwords, payment information, or any sort of account information. This tool is specifically used for fans of Monster Hunter Rise to play with one another more easily. Thank you and have fun!
